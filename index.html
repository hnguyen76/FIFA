Pattern playbook

Wager framework, not prediction theater.

Use these patterns as a decision layer before staking. Price, probability, and bankroll discipline matter more than picking the most famous team.

Pattern 01

Fade the single shortest outright

Favorites won 3 of 15 tournaments in the sample. Convert favorite conviction into smaller positions such as group winner, semi-final reach, or no-bet when price is thin.

Pattern 02

Target the 2nd-4th favorite band

This band captured many champions while avoiding the heaviest public-team premium. Require elite ranking, squad depth, and a route that does not force early coin-flips.

Pattern 03

Use longshots for place markets

Croatia and Morocco showed that deep runs can come from 33/1 to 200/1 teams. Outright tickets remain fragile; top-4, group qualification, and draw-no-bet markets fit the asymmetry better.

Pattern 04

Price the ranking gap

If a team is top 10 by FIFA points but priced outside the contender pack, inspect injuries, age curve, and draw. If nothing explains the gap, the market may be slow.

Risk policy

Responsible wager guardrails.

  • Stake in fixed units and cap total tournament exposure before the opening match.
  • Convert odds to implied probability, then bet only when your estimate clears the price.
  • Avoid chasing after shocks; World Cup variance is real and emotionally expensive.
  • Never treat historical patterns as guarantees. This dashboard is educational analysis.
